⏺ Research Findings: Google Business Profile Additional Statistics

  Based on my research using MCP Context7 and web search, YES - you can definitely include
  additional Google Business Profile statistics beyond just reviews. The Google Business
  Profile Performance API provides comprehensive analytics that would greatly enhance your
  RepliFast dashboard.

  Available Statistics Through Google Business Profile Performance API:

  📊 Core Visibility Metrics

  - Profile Views: Total unique views on Search and Maps (now counts each unique view per day)
  - Search Impressions: How often your profile appeared in search results
  - Discovery vs. Direct Searches: Breakdown of how customers found you
  - Search Keywords: Actual search terms customers used to find your business

  🎯 Customer Actions & Engagement

  - Website Clicks: Number of clicks to your website from your profile
  - Phone Calls: Click-to-call interactions from your profile
  - Direction Requests: Customers requesting directions to your location
  - Messages: Customer conversations initiated through your profile
  - Bookings: Completed bookings made through your profile
  - Product Views: Views on products listed in your profile

  📱 Platform & Device Analytics

  - Mobile vs. Desktop: Performance breakdown by device type
  - Google Search vs. Maps: Engagement across different Google platforms
  - Geographic Data: Where customers are discovering your business

  2025 Industry Benchmarks (from research):

  - 86% of views come from category-based searches (discovery)
  - Website visits account for 48% of all profile interactions
  - Phone calls represent 21% of interactions
  - Direction requests make up 9% of interactions
  - Average monthly profile views: 92.6K for hospitality industry
